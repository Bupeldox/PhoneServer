Proteins

One of my friends is very much a chemistry nerd, so i thought it would be nice to 3d print his favourite protein. These are some prototypes. I got the models off of something like jmol.

Tea Coasters

I like the look of tea stains on wood, but didn’t want to stain the furniture so I made some tea coasters out of an unused hazel walking stick. It's unvarnished so it can be stained by the tea. It might not be the most sanitary but i think it turned out pretty well.
